Slide 02 — Meet Priya

A Day in the Life
of an Applicant

Priya, a 21-year-old college student, needs a passport for her first international internship. She wakes up at 5 AM, travels 45 minutes, and joins a queue that snakes outside the building — only to realize she's missing a document.

By noon, she's made three trips between the counter and a xerox shop, filled a form with no instructions, and still isn't sure if her application is complete.

Slide 03 — Pain Points

What Really
Hurts the User

Behind every queue is a story of anxiety, wasted effort, and loss of trust. These aren't just inconveniences — they erode confidence in the system.

Unclear document checklist — applicants discover missing papers only at the counter
No time estimates — hours of waiting with zero communication or updates
Unhelpful staff — no one to guide through the form-filling process
Broken online portal — technical errors force in-person visits anyway
Slide 04 — Solution Journey

What If It
Just Worked?

Imagine Priya's experience reimagined: she checks documents online, books a slot, gets guided through every step — and walks out in under an hour with confidence.

Slide 05 — Point of View

A System That
Respects Your Time

By putting the user's emotional journey at the center of design — not just the process — we transform a stressful ordeal into a confident, efficient experience.
